Our Story

Rayleigh Nails Spa Salon has been a fixture on Rayleigh High Street for many years, serving both new and returning customers with warmth, skill, and dedication.

We are fully qualified professionals with extensive experience across nails, beauty, and wellness. Our team takes genuine pride in every treatment — from a quick polish change to a full afternoon of pampering.

Located just five minutes from Rayleigh Station, we are easy to reach and always ready to welcome you. We recommend booking ahead, especially at weekends, as we are a popular choice in the local area.

Whether you need acrylics, shellac, a facial, or a relaxing massage, we are here to fulfil your needs with care and attention to detail.
